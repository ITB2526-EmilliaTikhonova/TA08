How to reduce consumption

Electricity

  • Use LED lighting and switch off unused equipment.
  • Adjust heating and cooling setpoints by 1–2 ºC.
  • Schedule high-consumption tasks during daylight to maximize solar use.

Water

  • Install aerators on taps and low-flow fixtures.
  • Detect and repair leaks quickly.
  • Reduce irrigation and prioritize native plants.

Office consumables

  • Print only when necessary and use double-sided printing.
  • Promote digital documents instead of paper.
  • Reuse scrap paper for drafts and notes.

Cleaning consumables & services

  • Use concentrated and eco-labelled cleaning products.
  • Optimize cleaning schedules based on real occupancy.
  • Plan preventive maintenance to avoid inefficient equipment.
